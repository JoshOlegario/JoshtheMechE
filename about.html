About JoshtheMechE

Hi! I'm Josh Olegario, and I'm combining my engineering mindset with my love for great food.

My Background

I'm a proud alumnus of Arkansas State University, where I earned my degree in Mechanical Engineering. While my education taught me to analyze systems, solve problems, and appreciate precision, my real passion has always been food.

Why Food Reviews?

As a mechanical engineer, I'm wired to break things down, understand how they work, and evaluate performance. I bring that same analytical approach to food. Every dish is a system of flavors, textures, and techniques that work together (or don't). I review food with the same attention to detail I'd use to evaluate a machine.

What Makes My Reviews Different?

I don't just tell you if something tastes good. I break down:

  • Flavor Profile: Sweet, savory, spicy - how do the tastes balance?
  • Texture & Structure: Is it crispy when it should be? Tender? Perfectly cooked?
  • Value Engineering: Are you getting quality for your money?
  • Overall Performance: Would I come back? Would I recommend it?
